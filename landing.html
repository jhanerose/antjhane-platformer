Why Dream Jumper?

More than just a jumper

Six platform types, four power-ups, a relentless shadow boss, and gravity that fights back. No two runs are ever the same.

🪐
6 PLATFORM TYPES

Normal, Nightmare, Glitch, Moving, Spring, and Ice platforms — each with unique physics and surprises that keep every run fresh.

👾
SHADOW BOSS

A relentless shadow creeps from below, accelerating as you climb. Fall behind even a moment and the dream ends.

⚡
POWER-UP ITEMS

Collect Stars, Shields, Speed Boosts, and Coins with combo chains. Time your pickups to skyrocket your score.

Getting Started

How to Play

Easy to pick up, impossible to put down.

01
STEER SIDEWAYS

Use ← → or A D to move left and right. Rebind in Settings.

02
AUTO-BOUNCE

No jump button — your ant bounces automatically on every landing. Focus entirely on horizontal positioning.

03
GRAB ITEMS

Collect glowing power-ups hovering above platforms. Stars, shields, boosts, and coin combos can flip your run.

04
OUTRUN SHADOW

The Shadow Boss rises from below, getting faster over time. Keep climbing. If it catches you — game over.
